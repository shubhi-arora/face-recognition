Overview

The Recognito web application acts as an emotion detector, allowing people to identify their emotions by looking at their faces. By keeping suspects on this project's radar, the web app will serve as a truth-finder and distinguisher between object and face for the Cops.

Through its monitoring, this project will be able to observe every emotion the suspect exhibits. Every emotion will be tracked. After 24 hours, the most noted emotion will be displayed after this app has been keeping a watchful eye on culprits.

As an example-if, the most observed emotion is happiness, which means the culprit is not hiding anything and is confident that he is not wrong. When most observed emotion turns into fear, it likely means he has a secret and is not telling anyone. There is no doubt that these predictions are accurate because a person who is guilty will definitely become nervous, and this nervousness,fear will definitely be evident on his face.

It is almost impossible for a detective to constantly watch a criminal and his emotions, but this web app can do as much as it can. In addition, it eliminates the need to hire additional employees for observation.

Features:

  • It acts as a distinguisher between various objects and faces.
  • Whenever object other than a face is detected in line of sight of a camera , camera screen automatically gets freezed and unblocks only after face comes again in line of sight of camera.
  • Whenever there is a face it detects emotions and displays it.
  • Most observed emotion is displayed.
  • Other than the emotion race and gender of a person is displayed in result section.

Calculations:

  • Store each and every occurrence of emotion value into a map or dictionary.
  • Count the occurrenceof each emotion.
  • Traverse through the map and find the maximum occurence count emotion.
  • Display the result and proceed the same for race.
